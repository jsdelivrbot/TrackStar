Welcome to TrackStar!


Welcome to TrackStar, your new favorite vinyl record dealer. This is a company that prides itself on providing quality, vintage vinyl for modern people. In recent years, vinyl has made a comeback in a big way. There is a whole new generation of youngsters buying music specifically to listen to it on an age-old medium. There is nothing quite like the experience that listening to a classic record creates, and our fans are of the most passionate and devoted of music fanatics.

Beyond the over-priced, over-compressed and processed vinyl's being released by some record companies today, there are still thousands of records from the original golden age of music that are in perfect playing condition. Here at TrackStar, we help you find your favorite vinyl records from you favorite eras of music, each with their ethereal time stamp pressed into the groove marks.
